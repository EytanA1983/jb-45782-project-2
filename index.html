About this Project
A lightweight client-only crypto dashboard with a focus on speed, clarity, and zero backend.
It pulls live market data, lets you curate up to five coins, and streams their USD prices into a real‑time multi‑line chart.
Everything—from caching to state—is handled in the browser.
